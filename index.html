Hello, my name is Kimberly Juarez. I am currently majoring in Education at UC Riverside. I am a junior, and currently employed at Office Depot where I have been working for one year and ten months. My goal is to become a teacher for an elementary school or even middle school. Since I was young I have always dreamed of becoming a teacher, it is a passion. Eventually, I see myself as a principal or a counselor in administration. I just want to make an impact in students' lives just as the teachers I have done in my life. I want to motivate and encourage students to continue their education become successful and love what they are doing for their career. Although I am a full-time student I am also working as a part-time sales advisor at my local office supply store, Office Depot. So far I have enjoyed working here because it has taught me the value of social interaction. I have learned many skills during my time as a sales associate at Office Depot.
